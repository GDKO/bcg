Computational Biodiversity Research

The "Strengthening Computational Biodiversity Research in Greece" (Comp-Biodiv-GR) project is funded via the EU ERA Chair (HORIZON-WIDERA-2022-TALENTS-01: 2023-2028) program under project ID 101087081.

It started on 01.01.2023 and will last for 5 years.

The main goals of the project are to (i) advance computational biodiversity research in Crete, Greece, and beyond, (ii) to prevent brain-drain and stimulate brain-gain, and (iii) to trigger institutional reforms at FORTH and the Greek academic system in general.
