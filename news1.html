BCG setup phase starts

After a very sunny Christmas break in Crete, the BCG setup phase starts, job advertisments are being prepared, the web-site setup starts, and Alexandros (Alexis) Stamatakis has moved into the new offices at FORTH.

Projects initiatited prior to project start on January 1st 2023, such as the Malaise trap insect sampling in Southern Crete via a citizen science initiative will be continued (see drone picture of one of the traps above).

I am looking forward to the new challenges and getting the lab up and running - Prof. Dr. Alexandros (Alexis) Stamatakis - ERA chair holder and BCG group leader.

First contacts have been established to presenet the outreach program entitled "The Aegean Archipelago: a living laboratory of evolutionary biology" that was developed in Collaboration with the Natural History Museum of Crete at additional primary schools.
